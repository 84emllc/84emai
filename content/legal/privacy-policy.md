**Last Updated:** December 20, 2025

This privacy policy explains how 84EM.AI collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website.

## Information We Collect

84EM.AI is a personal blog and does not require account creation, login, or form submission to access content.

**Automatically Collected Information**

When you visit this site, our infrastructure providers may collect:

- Page views and referrer information (anonymized, no IP addresses stored)
- General geographic region (country-level only)
- Browser type and device category

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot identify individual visitors.

## How Information is Used

Any information collected is used solely to:

- Understand general traffic patterns and popular content
- Maintain site security and performance
- Improve the website experience

## Data Protection and Security

84EM.AI implements industry-standard security measures, including:

- HTTPS encryption for all connections
- DDoS protection and web application firewall
- Regular security monitoring

## Third-Party Service Providers

This site uses the following services to operate:

**Infrastructure**

- **Kinsta (Hosting):** Provides web hosting services. May process server logs for security and performance. [Privacy Policy](https://kinsta.com/legal/privacy-policy/)
- **Cloudflare (Security/CDN):** Provides security, performance optimization, and content delivery. [Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

**Analytics**

- **Simple Analytics:** Privacy-first analytics service that does not collect IP addresses, use cookies, or track any personally identifiable information. [Privacy Policy](https://simpleanalytics.com/privacy-policy)

**Development**

- **Claude Code:** AI-assisted development tool used to build and maintain this site. Claude Code processes code and development tasks only, not visitor data. [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

## Cookies

84EM.AI does not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes. Any cookies present are strictly functional, set by Cloudflare for security purposes.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

84EM.AI does not sell, share, rent, or disclose visitor information to third parties except:

- When required by law
- To protect the rights or safety of visitors or the site operator

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) including the right to:

- Access information collected about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of certain data processing

Since this site collects minimal anonymized data, most of these rights are satisfied by default.
